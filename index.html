Personal Projects

FormulaStats

FormulaStats is an iOS application I developed that allows users to search and compare any two drivers who have ever competed in Formula One. The app fetches data from the Ergast API using network calls and completion handlers. Written entirely in Swift, this project is still under development.

Weather App

During a 24-hour hackathon, I collaborated with a team to develop a real-time weather application. This app enables users to search their location and receive current weather data along with a weekly forecast. The project, written in Python, utilizes the National Weather Service API to retrieve real-time weather information.

City Biker

I participated in a 24 hour game jam at DIS Copenhagen. I worked with a team of four to create our game City Biker. City Biker is an endless obstacle avoidance game where the objective is to survive as long as possible. City Biker is developer in Unity using C#.

Home Alone: Lights Out

I worked with a team out 4 to develop Home Alone: Lights Out. This is an adventure horror game where you need to conquer your fears to drive the monsters from your home. Feel free to follow to link to play Home Alone: Lights out for yourself!

Escaped

I worked with a team out 4 to develop Escaped. Escaped is a first person 3D survival adventure game. You have to survive a crash landing on a remote island and a way to escape using the resources you can scavenge from the island.
